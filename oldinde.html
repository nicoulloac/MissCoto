Chile is a Gift

Why is Chile unique and different from other countries?

A letter for our families · Colegio Dunalastair

“

Wouldn't it be amazing to build a snowman, swim in the ocean, smell the exquisite essence of a green forest and visit a majestic volcano in just one single place? Is that even possible? Well, the answer is closer than you think. Just take a look around into your own country.

Chile's diverse landscapes, from icy Antarctica to the arid Atacama desert, make it an enticing destination. Its dramatic differences in customs, traditions and lifestyles — depending on the different geographical zones — are a significant pretext for tourists wanting to come back to our country every time they can.

But, how much of it do you really know? How much do you know about the country you live in? Would you be able to convince your family members to visit our country during the next holidays? Which highlights, activities and suggestions would you give to someone who would like to travel around Chile?

Our Learning Route

Three milestones that guide students from research to a final creative product.

SP1

Sub Product 1

A poster of a Chilean zone and region, paired with a protection protocol manual for a natural hazard of that area.

SP2

Sub Product 2

A tourism poster featuring an itinerary of a destination in Chile worth visiting.

FP

Final Product

A video or blog that integrates Sub Product 1 and Sub Product 2 into one promotional piece.
